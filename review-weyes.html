Album Review: Titanic Rising - Weyes Blood

Titanic Rising is cinematic and immersive, blending soft-rock textures with modern songwriting and a widescreen, dreamlike atmosphere. Weyes Blood’s voice is clear and steady, floating above arrangements that feel both nostalgic and futuristic at the same time. The album moves slowly but never drags, and the production creates a strong emotional pull from track to track. It’s an album that rewards focused listening and feels cohesive from beginning to end. Overall, it’s a beautiful, carefully crafted record that feels timeless and current at once.

My three favorite songs each highlight a different side of the album: “Something to Believe” feels sweeping and hopeful, “Andromeda” is dreamy and melodic, and “A Lot Is Gonna Change” delivers one of the most haunting moments on the record.
